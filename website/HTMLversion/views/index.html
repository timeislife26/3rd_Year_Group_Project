Vision Statement

A societal transformation is taking place whereby healthcare professionals, and patients alike, are shifting from reactive to predictive HealthAI care management.
Rather than waiting for health problems to be manifested as lifethreatening conditions, AI technology enables the prediction of future health problems using datasets of patient information.
Enter LYFERISK, an AI-trained model that will assist doctors and patient alike to more quickly diagnose impeding issues and take action faster and more efficiently thus increasing the chances of success for treatment.
